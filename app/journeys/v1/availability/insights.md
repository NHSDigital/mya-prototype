## Why this journey exists

Users think about availability at different zoom levels: "what's on today", "how's this week
looking", "is the month covered". The day, week and month views answer each of those without the
user having to reconstruct the picture themselves.

## Key decisions

- The three views share one secondary navigation so switching scale is a single click.
- Past days are shown but not editable, so history stays visible without inviting changes to it.

## What to try next

- Check whether users expect to act (cancel/change) directly from the week and month views.
