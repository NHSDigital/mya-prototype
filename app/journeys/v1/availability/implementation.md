## User story

**As a** site administrator
**I want to** view availability by day, week or month
**So that** I can understand coverage at the level of detail I need.

## Acceptance criteria

- **Given** I am viewing availability
  **When** I switch between day, week and month
  **Then** the same clinics are shown at the chosen scale, with totals where relevant.

- **Given** a day is in the past
  **When** I view it
  **Then** its clinics are visible but not editable.
