## User story

**As a** site administrator
**I want to** see all of a site's clinics in one place
**So that** I can quickly find and manage the right clinic.

## Acceptance criteria

- **Given** a site has clinics
  **When** I open the clinics list
  **Then** I see each clinic with its services and a link to change it.

- **Given** there are more clinics than fit on one page
  **When** I move to the next page
  **Then** the remaining clinics are shown.
